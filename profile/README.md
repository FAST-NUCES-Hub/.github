## 🌟 About FAST-Hub

**FAST-Hub** is the official organization powering [**DASTI**](https://dasti.vercel.app), a community-driven platform designed by and for the students of FAST NUCES (Karachi Campus). 

Our mission is simple: **to provide a unified, accessible, and comprehensive repository of study materials so every student has the resources they need to ace their exams.** 

### Why an Organization?
Previously, study materials were scattered across private drives, WhatsApp groups, or tied dependently to individual personal accounts that could become inactive. **FAST-Hub** was created to decentralize ownership and build a true community platform. By structuring our resources into a dedicated organization:
- **No Single Point of Failure:** Repositories belong to the community, not just one person; the owner [(me)](https://github.com/MuxammilSidd) got his account banned once.
- **Automated Pipelines:** The DASTI GitHub App automatically routes contributions to the correct semester repository.
- **Open Contribution:** Anyone can submit materials via our beautiful web UI, ensuring the archives grow continuously through community effort.

## ⚡ How to Contribute

You do not need to fork these repositories manually! We've made contributing effortless:

1. Visit [**dasti.vercel.app/contribute**](https://dasti.vercel.app/contribute)
2. Select your course, material type, and upload your files (up to 100MB!).
3. The **Team DASTI** bot will automatically generate the correct metadata, open a pull request, and merge it here.
4. Climb the [Leaderboard](https://dasti.vercel.app/leaderboard) as you help your fellow students!
